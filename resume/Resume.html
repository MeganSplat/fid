About Me

Hi there! My name is Megan, I am a graphic design student. I started out drawing Illustrations on my iPad using Procreate. I learned how to use it pretty well but wanted to learn more about Adobe products, so I enrolled back in school at the age of 30! I have been pertty creative most of my life, I used to write stories and draw illustrations as early as pre-school. I love Legos, even as an adult. I used to spend hours building things. I enjoy acrylic painting and drawing portraits. My mom and grandmother taught me how to crochet when I was in elementary school. I love to cook and bake, I even ocassionally sell "treats" like chocolate covered strawberries, oreos, cookies, cakes, etc... I am even a licensed nail technician. Over all, my favorite thing to do is make something from scratch, whether it be food or art, and see people enjoying it. My ultimate goal is to be a business owner and to just make any kind of art that makes people happy.
