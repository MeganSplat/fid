WHY WE DO WHAT WE DO

The English Bulldog Rescue is a volunteer group whose mission is to rehab and re-home neglected, abused and unwanted English Bulldogs. We also provide a safe alternative to shelters for owners that are faced with the decision to find a new home for their bulldog.

We rely solely on donations to finance veterinary exams, inoculations, medications and surgeries, as well as the daily feeding and care that our rescued dogs need while in our care.

Florida English Bulldog Rescue has been helping this wonderful breed since 2020. We are a small group of volunteers with a huge love for the bulldog. We are a 501(c)3 organization, that has helped over 1000 bulldogs and that number is growing. We give each bulldog that enters our program 100% care and rehab so that it has the best chance at a quality life in a forever home.
